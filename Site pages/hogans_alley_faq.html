Shipping

  • We currently ship throughout Canada. We're working on expanding to the US and international markets — join our newsletter to be notified when we do.

  • Free shipping on all orders within Canada. No minimum purchase required.

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Once shipped:

    • British Columbia: 2–3 business days
    • Alberta, Saskatchewan, Manitoba: 3–5 business days
    • Ontario, Quebec: 4–6 business days
    • Atlantic provinces: 5–7 business days
    • Northern territories: 7–10 business days

  • You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order ships. You can also track your order by contacting us at hello@hogansalley.ca.

  • Not at this time. We ship all orders via Canada Post Expedited Parcel, which provides a good balance of speed and reliability with full tracking.

Returns & Exchanges

  • We offer 30-day returns on all unworn items with tags attached. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return your jacket for a full refund or exchange.

  • Email us at hello@hogansalley.ca with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours.

    Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3–5 business days.

  • Yes. We provide prepaid return labels for all Canadian returns. Just pack your jacket in its original packaging (or similar), attach the label, and drop it off at any Canada Post location.

  • Absolutely. Email us at hello@hogansalley.ca with your order number and the size you need. If the new size is in stock, we'll ship it as soon as we receive your return — or immediately if you prefer (we'll just need a card on file).

    Not sure which size? Check our Size Guide or reach out — we're happy to help.

  • We stand behind our products. If you receive a defective jacket or notice a manufacturing issue, contact us immediately at hello@hogansalley.ca with photos. We'll send a replacement right away — no return required.

  • Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of receiving your return. It may take an additional 5–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank.

Product Questions

  • Our jackets are water-resistant with a DWR (Durable Water Repellent) finish that causes water to bead up and roll off. They're perfect for light to moderate rain — the kind you encounter walking around the city. For heavy downpours or extended exposure, you may eventually get some moisture through.

    The DWR can be reactivated with heat (tumble dry low) and refreshed with spray-on treatments. See our Care Guide for details.

  • Shell: 88% Recycled Polyester, 12% Spandex (110 gsm) with DWR finish

    Lining: 94% Recycled Polyester face, 100% Recycled Polyester back (320 gsm) — a soft, matte recycled PET fabric, not fleece

    Both fabrics are OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified. Each jacket uses approximately 27 recycled plastic bottles.

  • No. Our artwork is sublimation printed, which means the dye becomes part of the fabric itself rather than sitting on top. It won't crack, peel, or fade with washing. The colours will stay vibrant for the life of the jacket.

  • Machine wash cold (30°C), gentle cycle, with mild liquid detergent. Close all zippers, turn inside out. Tumble dry low to reactivate the DWR coating. No fabric softener, no bleach, no dry cleaning.

    For complete care instructions, see our Care Guide.

  • Our jackets have a relaxed, unisex fit designed for layering. Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable layered fit.

    Still unsure? Email us at hello@hogansalley.ca with your measurements and we'll help you find the perfect fit.

  • Our jackets are made in Canada using domestic production. The artwork is created locally in Vancouver, and each piece is cut and sewn by skilled Canadian workers.
