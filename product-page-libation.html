About this pattern ▼

This print started as a sketch during a late-night gathering — the kind where drinks are shared, music gets louder, and stories turn into folklore. The pattern layers geometric shapes inspired by traditional drinking vessels, celebration motifs, and the rhythm of people moving together.

The warm yellows and deep reds reference firelight and spice, while the green anchors everything back to earth. It's designed to feel like energy in motion: loud, warm, and unapologetically alive.

Every jacket tells a story. This one celebrates connection.
