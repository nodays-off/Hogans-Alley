Celebrating the sacred ritual of gathering. Inspired by the calabash gourd — vessel of communion, symbol of shared abundance across West African tradition.

The Story

The Inspiration

The Calabash Speaks

In Yoruba tradition, the calabash represents the cosmos — the top half the sky, the bottom half the earth, and where they meet: the realm of humanity and its celebrations. The Libation print draws from this symbolism.

Colour & Meaning

Swirling Reds & Golds

Swirling reds and golds evoke palm wine at sunset. Deep greens honor the land that provides. The spiral patterns trace the eternal cycle of giving and receiving.

Whether you're navigating Borough Market's Saturday crowd or sharing appetizers at a friend's opening, this jacket understands that some of life's best moments happen around abundance.

Worn Worldwide

From Market to Market

Granville Island. Borough Market. Alberta Arts District. The Libation print finds its natural home wherever people gather to share food, stories, and the simple pleasure of each other's company.

The calabash gourds, the flowing spirals, the geometric blocks — all working in conversation, just like the people who wear it.
