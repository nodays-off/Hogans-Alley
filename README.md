## üé® AI Image Generation Tips

**For all images, maintain:**
- Cool color temperature for environments
- Jackets as warmest/brightest element
- Overcast/rainy atmosphere
- Wet surfaces and reflections
- Urban settings (bus stops, skateparks, streets)
- Diverse cast (age, ethnicity, style)
- Natural, not over-posed
- Documentary street photography style

**Specific settings:**
- Adults: Ages 16-25, streetwear aesthetic
- Kids: Ages 5-10, playful energy
- Time of day: Overcast daytime or moody evening
- Weather: Light rain to heavy drizzle
- Locations: Vancouver, Seattle, London vibes
