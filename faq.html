Getting It to You

We ship to Canada, the United States, and the United Kingdom. All orders are dispatched from our studio in Vancouver, BC.

Shipping is complimentary on all orders. The price you see is the price you pay.

Canadian orders typically arrive within 3–5 business days. US orders take 5–8 business days. UK orders take 7–12 business days. Once shipped, you'll receive tracking via email.

Return

If It's Not Right

Returns & Exchanges

Email us at hello@hogansalley.com with your order number. We'll send you a prepaid return label. Returns are free within Canada — international returns ship at cost.

Need a different size? Same process. We'll coordinate the swap or process a full refund if your size is unavailable.

Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of receiving your return. If your jacket arrives damaged or defective, we replace it immediately.

The Jacket

Product & Care

Our jackets feature a DWR finish that causes water to bead and roll off. Designed for urban rain — commutes, markets, casual walks.

Shell: 88% recycled polyester, 12% spandex. Lining: 100% recycled polyester with soft matte fleece-back. Both OEKO-TEX certified.

The print is digitally bonded into the fabric — it won't crack, peel, or fade. Machine wash cold, tumble dry low. Visit our Size Guide for fit details.
